b) Command Data

c) Optional Data

    Third-party Integrations: If you link any third-party services to the bot (e.g., Spotify, YouTube), we may access data provided by these services to deliver features. This data is not stored by us but is used in real-time for functionality.

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

We retain data only for as long as necessary to provide the Bot’s services or as required by law. If you disconnect or uninstall the bot, we will delete any personal data collected within a reasonable time frame, unless otherwise required to retain it for legal purposes.
